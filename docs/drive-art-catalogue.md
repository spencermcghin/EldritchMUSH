## 4. Gaps & Notes

**Enemy art coverage vs. game enemies**
- The DB currently has **only "zombie"** as an enemy. The Bestiary has **no "zombie"** asset by name — the closest substitutes are **Bones_ (×4), Revenant, Wight, Withered_L** (undead). A zombie render would need to be commissioned or re-tagged from the Bones/Withered art.
- Quests reference **Crow bandits** (and "Crow" / Corveaux-style raven motifs). There is **no dedicated bandit/Crow-bandit creature art**. Substitutes: `Cards/raven.png` + `raven1.png` (raven motif), Corveaux house crest (corvid theme), and `Vigil/Raven.png`. A human-bandit portrait would have to be drawn from `Archetypes/Watercolor character` (e.g. Urchin/Hunter) — none are explicitly "bandit."
- **No goblin, orc, troll, giant, dragon-creature, or generic "beast" portraits** exist (Aragon's dragon is heraldry, not a creature render). The bestiary is undead/fae/lycanthrope/witch-heavy.
- **Hexen** (the witch-hunt enemy faction) has emblems (ring sigils, witch-hunter markers) but **no full antagonist figure** — its human enemies would reuse the Witch/hag or Blayne-templar mood-board.

**Asset-quality caveats**
- The `Inspiration/House *` folders are **downloaded web reference images** (Pinterest-style hashed filenames, GoT screenshots), **not original game art** — usable for mood/direction only, likely not licensed for shipping.
- The most useful UI-scale assets are PNG; many map/banner masters are **20–128 MB** TIF/JPG/AI and must be downscaled for web.
- **Frames/borders:** there is **no dedicated frame/border folder**. The only ready-made frame is `medic_blank.png`; the Tarot `back.jpg` and Bestiary `*_floatingpage` variants are the other frame-like options. An encounter-card frame would likely need to be built.

**Non-art / housekeeping items found in the tree (flagged, not assets)**
- `Invoices/` (5 PDFs) and stray `Rechnung_*_Kozar.pdf` in **Creatures/** and **Aragon/** are German vendor invoices.
- `.DS_Store 2` entries in **Map/** and **Item Card graphics/** are macOS junk.
- `EldritchPicks/` and `Print Books/` are **empty**.
- `Character Art/Other Art/` (Queen of Arnesse, King Giles) and root `Scroll_print_final.pdf` / `Schematics Eldritch.pptx` are documents, not images.

**Cross-references worth noting for encounter building**
- Aragon's **dragon** and Cirque's **Ouroboros** are the two strongest unused creature/cult symbols.
- Event-9 backdrops (Plague, Burial, Fae Feast, Mist's Mysteries) align with horror/fae encounters and can serve as telegraph splash art.
- The World Map Icons `threat*/threatchampion*/encounter*` tokens are **purpose-built for a graphical encounter/strategic map** and map directly onto a combat-encounter feature.
